📖 Motivation

Learning is essential for all human beings. It plays a vital role in our lives. Everyone, without exception, needs to learn — even a teacher or trainer remains a learner in some way or another.

Right now, I am addressing you through these lines, and it is very important to understand how to build a strong foundation for our academic and spiritual future. We must stay warm, excited, and motivated every single day.

📘 The Importance of Learning

"فَإِنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي نَفْسِهِ، ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي نَفْسِي، وَإِنْ ذَكَرَنِي فِي مَلَإٍ، ذَكَرْتُهُ فِي مَلَإٍ خَيْرٍ مِنْهُمْ"
"If he remembers Me in himself, I remember him in Myself. And if he remembers Me in a gathering, I remember him in a gathering better than theirs."
— [Sahih al-Bukhari & Sahih Muslim]

🏅 The Best Among You

"خيركم من تعلم القرآن وعلمه"
“The best among you are those who learn the Qur'an and teach it.”
— [Sahih Al-Bukhari 5027]

🎁 Countless Rewards

"من قرأ حرفًا من كتاب لله فله به حسنة، والحسنة بعشر أمثالها"
“Whoever recites a letter from the Book of Allah will be credited with a good deed, and a good deed gets a tenfold reward.”
— [Sunan At-Tirmidhi 2910]
